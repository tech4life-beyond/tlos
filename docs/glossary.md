# Glossary

## Canonical terms

### TLOS
Tech4Life Operating System: the governance and doctrine standards that define *how* Tech4Life creates and stewards technology.

### TOIL
Tech4Life Open Impact License: the legal framework that defines usage, commercial pathways, and enforcement posture.

### Product
A named, scoped technology/system/device/process that is created and stewarded under Tech4Life governance and (when published) TOIL.

### Product Pack
The structured artifact bundle for a product (folders + documents) governed by factory rules and validated by the pipeline.

### Product Registry
The canonical machine-readable index of products and their identifiers/status, published from `product-registry`.

### Product ID (TOIL ID)
The canonical identifier assigned to a product under TOIL governance (e.g., `T4L-TOIL-###-SLUG`).

### Release Gate
A composite pass/fail policy that determines whether a product may be published or commercially licensed, including legal, ethics, technical, and registry checks.

### Normative
A statement that defines a requirement. Use keywords: **MUST / MUST NOT / SHOULD / MAY**.

### Informative
A statement that explains context, rationale, examples, or guidance, without creating an obligation.

### Contributor / Creator / Architect
Role levels in the ecosystem (definitions live in `docs/contributor-levels/`); avoid using these terms interchangeably.

### Steward
A responsible party who maintains the integrity of a product or standard over time.
